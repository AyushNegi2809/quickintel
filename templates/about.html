About QuickIntel

QuickIntel is an AI-powered document intelligence suite designed to simplify how users interact with text, PDFs, and Excel files. We believe in merging the power of Artificial Intelligence with simplicity and accessibility.

Our Mission

To make document understanding effortless - empowering users to analyze, summarize, and enhance content in seconds.
